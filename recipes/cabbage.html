Norwegian Cabbage

Description:

Delicious cabbage, sweet and sour. Goes good with anything meaty.

Sourkraut-y with lots of caraway seeds

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 1 large cabbage, cored and shredded
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon caraway seed
  • 1/2 cup of white wine vinegar

Steps:

  1. Lay bacon in bottom of pot. Cover with cabbage. Sprinkle flour and caraway seed. Repeat layers until run out of room.
  2. Fill pot until 3/4 up side. Bring to a boil and cook for 45 minutes. Let cool, then stir in vinegar and sugar. Done!
