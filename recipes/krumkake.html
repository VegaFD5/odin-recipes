Krumkake

Description:

Crispy, mild, vanilla flavoured wafer. Similar to the texture of an ice cream cone.

Very Norwegian, fill with cream and chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Heat the krumkake iron over medium heat.
  2. Cream together butter and sugar in bowl. Add eggs, mix well with spoon. Pour in milk and vanilla, mix well.
  3. Place teaspoon of batter on iron, and press together. Cook until brown, 30 seconds per side. Remove from iron and quickly roll around a stick or cone before they harden.
