Pancakes

Description:

Quick simple and easy. Can roll up and eat like a jacky jack.

Serve with cream, jam and blueberries or salt and tomato sauce for a savory option!

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar

Steps

  1. Combine eggs and milk and blend.
  2. Add flour, salt, and sugar and blend until smooth.
  3. Heat pan over high heat. Scoop 1/4 batter into pan and tilt til batter coats bottom. Cook until top looks dry. Flip and cook other side. Remove to plate and repeat.
